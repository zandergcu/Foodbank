Volunteering

We have plenty of volunteer roles available!

Our volunteers are responsible for assuring we maintain a safe, friendly and efficient workspace, all while making our clients and colleagues feel welcomed.

As a volunteer, we train you to become well versed in stock rotation, store maintenance and dealing with the public.

We pride ourselves on being flexible and understanding regarding our volunteer’s skills and requirements, there’s always a job for everyone.

If you are interested in volunteering with Drumchapel Food Bank please contact- info@drumfoodbank.co.uk or give us a call on: 0141 944 3335
