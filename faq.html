Frequently Asked Questions

  • Helping Us

  • How do I become a volunteer?

    Check out our volunteer page for an idea of the roles available and the days they help out on. Then get in touch with us to get registered and help out! Note: As alot of the work could involve working with vulnerable people, some checks and forms will be required before being signed off as a volunteer.

  • Can I bring cooked food as a donation?

    Not really. But get in contact with us and we will have a talk about it.

  • Do yous accept clothes as donations?

    Not really. But get in contact with us and we will have a talk about it.

  • How often can you volunteer?

    As often as you like. We operate Monday-Friday and each day involves different work.

  • Seeking Help

  • How can I use the foodbank?

    You will need to bring a referral to us in order to use the Foodbank. These can be granted from your local job centre.

  • I'm not from Drumchapel, can I still use it?

    Yes, on the condition that your referral is to us.

  • Do yous offer other additional services?

    Aswell as giving out food on the day of operation we have an emergency service where people can collect emergency supplies from our office on other days. We also under certain circumstances can offer power/gas support. Check out our useful links section at the bottom of the page for additional support from other organisations.

  • Other

  • What type of stuff do yous currently need the most?

    Check out our Facebook or Twitter for an up to date list of what we need most.

  • Do yous post on social media?

    Yes we have Facebook and Twitter. You can find the links at the bottom of the page. Please do connect with us!

  • Is there any additional ways I can help?

    We are always looking for help in supporting our local people in crisis. Please do get in touch if you would like to discuss further ways of helping us help others.

  • Do yous have a method of setting up regular donations?

    This can be arranged and would be greatly appreciated. Please get in contact with us for security reasons as it would most likely be arranged through a direct debit bank method.
